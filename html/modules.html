 Caching APIsThe caching CMM caches the results of encryption and decryption operations to reduce the number of calls made to backing keyrings (e.g. KMS)
 Encryption Context utilitiesThe encryption context is a string-to-string map of contextual information that is cryptographically bound to the message. We represent the encryption context as an aws_hash_table mapping from aws_string * to aws_string *; for the most part, aws_hash_table methods are used to manipulate these structures, but we provide some higher-level helper methods in this section. These helpers will mostly be of interest to developers of custom CMMs or keyrings

 Materials providersThe behavior of the encryption SDK is largely defined by two types of materials providers - the Crypto Materials Managers (CMMs) and Keyrings. In order to perform an encryption or decryption operation, the Session APIs must be given a CMM, which will typically be configured to delegate to one or more keyrings to perform the key wrapping or unwrapping operation
